What is the Dream Act?

  • DREAM stands for Development, Relief, and Education for Alien Minors
  • This Bill provides residency to specific immigrants.
    • Requirements:
    • Entered the country before AGE 16
    • Have an american high school diploma or GED
    • Are 35 years old or younger
    • Have no criminal record
  • In return they get:
    • To complete 1 2-year degree
    • OR 2 years in the military
